NotPurple Affinity

Be a part of notpurple in your own way. Display one of these images on your web pages. It's free and it's pointless!

To use any of these buttons, just copy the html that follows them into your web page to get a nice, notpurple, clickable button of your own. You're welcome to copy the images to your own web page if you prefer.
